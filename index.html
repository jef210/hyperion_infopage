About Us

Hyperion Wholesale LLC., is Denver’s unmatched full-service, locally-owned DSD. It all began with a single product, distributed out of Jake's home in 2010, and has now grown to hundreds of products and over 150 customers, distributed out of our 10,000 sq./ft. warehouse located along the Broadway Corridor, in Downtown Denver.

We are privileged to be a full service DSD and Wholesale distributor in the Front Range of Colorado. We pride ourselves in our top-notch customer service, our product guarantees, and the product brands we represent.

What We Do

It is often said that, “the Devil is in the details,” and here at Hyperion Wholesale, the details are what we do. We know that you’re busy with the day-to-day operation of your business, the countless tasks that are part and parcel of finding success in a highly competitive industry, which is why we help with the rest. Full-Service Distribution that is worry-free will help you to operate more efficiently, maximizing your potential, and increasing your profits.
