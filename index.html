About Us

Hyperion Wholesale LLC., is Denver’s unmatched full-service, locally-owned DSD. It all began with a single product, distributed out of Jake's home in 2010, and has now grown to hundreds of products and over 150 customers, distributed out of our 10,000 sq./ft. warehouse located along the Broadway Corridor, in Downtown Denver.

We are privileged to be a full service DSD and Wholesale distributor in the Front Range of Colorado. We pride ourselves in our top-notch customer service, our product guarantees, and the product brands we represent.

What We Do

We know that you’re busy with the day-to- day operation of your business, with the countless tasks that are part and parcel of finding success in a highly competitive industry, which is For retailers, our Full-Service Distribution is worry-free and will help you to operate more efficiently, maximizing your potential, and increasing your profits.

Here's a few things we can do for you:

Category Management:

Need to ensure that you have the right product on the shelf? We take care of that, monitoring and charting local trends, demographics and sales, insuring that you have the right product in place for the right location.

Merchandising:

Need to ensure that you’ve got the right product in the right place on the shelf? We do that too. Our sales staff is our delivery staff, which means your bi-weekly or weekly visit is a hands-on evaluation of what products are moving and which ones are not. If a product isn’t working where it’s at, then we will personally find the correct product for the space or will leverage our buying power to find sales solutions. Hyperion Wholesale knows how valuable your space is, and so do you, which is why we are committed to making sure that you’ve the right product, at the right price, in the right place to maximize profits.

Marketing:

Would you love to market some of your products but find that the time and cost make sampling and demonstrating products unfeasible? Let us do the work. Hyperion Wholesale is constantly communicating with our manufacturing partners seeking out cost effective ways to sample and demo our many fantastic products. Hyperion Wholesale knows that one of the keys to success in retail is communication with the customer, and we’ve many strategies for making sure that our products and brands speak to your customers like no other.

Routing:

Need to ensure that your deliveries are made in a timely, consistent and professional fashion? At Hyperion Wholesale, we believe that Timeliness is next to Awesomeness. We know that time is money, especially when you’ve got staffing, retail, sales, and a host of other concerns to worry about, which is why you can rely on us to be consistent and efficient.

Product Guarantee:

All Hyperion products are fully guaranteed against packaging and and product defects. Any issues with the product or packaging will be replaced free of charge.

Sales Promise:

Hyperion Wholesale offers a unique money-back guarantee on the products we warehouse that do not meet your sales expectations. If any of our products aren’t performing to your needs, we will gladly take them back for a full exchange or refund.

Manufacturing Partners:

If you’re one of the many of hundreds of Colorado Proud Companies that is looking for a distribution partner, this is who we are: Hyperion Wholesale is a full-service DSD that is currently serving over 150 different retail locations in the Denver Metropolitan Area. The businesses that we serve run the gamut of retail sellers. From convenience stores to health food grocers, and everything in between.

 

We have an energetic and motivated sales team that is constantly pursuing new leads in order to further expand our reach into the Front Range market and we expect double-digit percentage growth in customers in 2016. We bring this energy to bear for each and every one of our manufacturing partners.

 

What we do. Hyperion Wholesale is a full-service distributor that puts a premium on customer service. As part of our full-service offering we do all the little things that our retail partners love. For starters, we maintain and stock the premier shelf space that our retailers offer to us, giving them a worry-free experience at both the stock and inventory site of the sale.

 

What else? We personally visit each store regularly, to inform, relate, and generate interest in the exclusive products that we offer. We also offer a unique sampling and demo program, that promotes multiple products and lines, to drive our retail customers to your products and generate outstanding sales. In short, we are a distributor that represents your brand, like it is our brand, and we’d love an opportunity to show you how this model drives sales.

 

Here at Hyperion Wholesale we are looking for more than products, we are looking for Colorado Proud companies to partner with so that we can offer our retailers a full line of fantastic, local products!
