About Us

Hyperion Wholesale LLC., is Denver’s unmatched full-service, locally-owned DSD. It all began with a single product, distributed out of Jake's home in 2010, and has now grown to hundreds of products and over 150 customers, distributed out of our 10,000 sq./ft. warehouse located along the Broadway Corridor, in Downtown Denver.

We are privileged to be a full service DSD and Wholesale distributor in the Front Range of Colorado. We pride ourselves in our top-notch customer service, our product guarantees, and the product brands we represent.

What We Do

It is often said that, “the Devil is in the details,” and here at Hyperion Wholesale, the details are what we do. We know that you’re busy with the day-to-day operation of your business, the countless tasks that are part and parcel of finding success in a highly competitive industry, which is why we help with the rest. Full-Service Distribution that is worry-free will help you to operate more efficiently, maximizing your potential, and increasing your profits.

Category Management?- Having the right product for the right location. (demographics and lifestyles)

Merchandising? (Putting products in the right place, in the right qty., at the right price)Hyperion Wholesale knows how valuable your space is, and so do you, which is why we are committed to making sure that you’ve the right product, at the right price, in the right place to maximize profits.

Marketing? Our partners and our sampling program.

Hyperion Wholesale knows that one of the keys to success in retail is communication with the customer, and we’ve many strategies for making sure that our products speak to your customers like no other.

Routing? How are we superior?

Timeliness is next to Awesomness. We know that time is money, especially when you’ve got staffing, retail, sales, and a host of other concerns to worry about, which is why you can rely on us to be consistent and efficient.

Product Guarantee:

All Hyperion products are fully guaranteed against packaging and and product defects. Any issues with the product or packaging will be replaced free of charge.

Sales Promise:

Hyperion Wholesale offers a unique money-back guarantee on the products we warehouse that do not meet your sales expectations. If any of our products aren’t performing to your needs, we will gladly take them back for a full exchange or refund.
